## Josef Müller-Brockmann, Musica Viva concert posters (1955–1960s)

The canonical reference for the Swiss International Typographic Style. Left-aligned grotesque type, asymmetric grid composition, single spot colour, no ornament. The precise move the `swiss-editorial` token is trying to elicit.
