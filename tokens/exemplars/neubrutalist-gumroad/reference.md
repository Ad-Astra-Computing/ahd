# Exemplars · neubrutalist-gumroad

Neubrutalism on the web has no classical public-domain analogue; it borrows from 1960s Eastern European political poster design and 1990s zine aesthetics. These references are the lineage.

## Soviet/Polish constructivist poster design (pre-1929 public domain)

Heavy black borders, flat primary blocks, sharp offset shadows, geometric type.

- Source: Library of Congress, Prints & Photographs Division, Russian Political Posters
- URL: https://www.loc.gov/pictures/search/?q=Russian+political+poster&sp=1
- Date: 1917–1929 (pre-1929 works are public domain in the US)
- Licence: Public domain (pre-1929)
