# Exemplars · manual-sf

The `manual-sf` token targets the editorial modernist lineage Manual SF works in (Söhne + Tiempos, oversized photography, one spot, asymmetric 12-col grid). The studio's own work is under copyright; public-domain exemplars come from the lineage Manual draws on.
