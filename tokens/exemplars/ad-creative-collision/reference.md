# Exemplars · ad-creative-collision

Agency collision aesthetics (Wieden+Kennedy, Sagmeister, DIA) are contemporary and under copyright. The public-domain lineage is 1960s agency work and Soviet constructivist poster design.

## Doyle Dane Bernbach, VW "Think Small" campaign (1959)

The founding text of the modern agency aesthetic — typography-forward, minimal image, confrontational copy.

- Source: Advertising Educational Foundation archive
- URL: https://aef.com/advertising-industry/ddb-think-small/
- Author: Helmut Krone (art director), Julian Koenig (copywriter)
- Date: 1959
- Licence: Under copyright but widely reproduced in academic context. Reference by URL.
